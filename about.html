About COOLAB

Turning culture
into edible art.

From stories and design to 3D ice cream creations — COOLAB transforms ideas into sweet experiences that connect people, art, and flavour.

Why We Started

Founded in Edinburgh in 2025, COOLAB began with one question — what if culture could be tasted? Inspired by cultural research and Scottish heritage, we transform stories and design into 3D ice cream creations.

How We’re Doing

Since launch, COOLAB has grown from a small studio into a creative food brand featured in festivals, events, and venues across Edinburgh, collaborating with local partners such as Edinburgh Castle, Camera Obscura & World of Illusions, and the Edinburgh Folks & Food Festival. Our products are praised for their originality and quality.

Why Choose Us

As Scotland’s only bespoke 3D ice cream producer, we combine artistic storytelling with food innovation. Our eco-friendly bamboo sticks, Scottish dairy, and clean ingredients reflect our commitment to sustainability and honesty.

  • 🎨 Bespoke 3D designs inspired by culture
  • 🍦 Locally sourced ingredients and Scottish dairy
  • 🌿 Sustainable materials and packaging
  • 🤝 Collaboration with creative partners and festivals

Our Vision

To make creativity edible — turning cultural stories into sweet experiences that connect people, ideas, and communities. COOLAB believes food is not just taste, but a language of imagination.
