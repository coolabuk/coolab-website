Our Story

COOLAB began in Edinburgh, born from the idea that ice cream should taste like real ingredients — not artificial flavourings.

What started as a small kitchen experiment became a micro ice cream lab focused on honest, small-batch production. We slow-cook our bases, use seasonal produce from local suppliers, and constantly test new pairings. We’re not chasing mass production — just flavour that feels personal.

Our Philosophy

COOLAB stands for curiosity, care, and craft. We believe food should connect people, not just fill shelves.

  • 🍦 Small-batch only — quality over quantity.
  • 🌿 Real ingredients — fruit, milk, and cream, not syrups.
  • 🧊 Craft process — hand-cooked bases and custom textures.
  • 🤝 Collaboration — we grow by creating together.
Slogan

“Less factory. More flavour.”

Every scoop tells a story — crafted by hand, shared with care.
