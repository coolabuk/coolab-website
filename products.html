Our Creations

COOLAB designs and produces creative 3D ice cream in Edinburgh. We turn stories, heritage and visual identity into edible objects — for festivals, venues, launches and cultural experiences.

3D Ice Cream Popsicle

Sculpted, handheld 3D ice creams on eco-friendly bamboo sticks. Each design is inspired by cultural icons — architecture, symbols, characters, heritage — and produced as a limited run.

Perfect for: festivals, pop-ups, museum shops, brand activations, tourism experiences, collectibles.

Plated 3D Ice Cream Dessert

A restaurant-style 3D ice cream composition designed for plate service. Structured forms, layered textures, custom flavour profiles — presented as a complete dessert.

Perfect for: venue menus, dining experiences, private events, tasting menus, collaborations.
